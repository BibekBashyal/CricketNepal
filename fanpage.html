Rhino Fans


Known as the Rhino Fans, Nepal has one of the most passionate fan-base outside the full members. Each international home match gathers a crowd of 8,000 - 10,000. Some of the more crucial matches have had estimated attendance of more than 20,000. National televisions regularly broadcast live matches held in Nepal and often from abroad. Since 2012 they have started broadcasting some domestic matches as well.
